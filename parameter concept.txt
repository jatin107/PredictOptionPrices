 ELM: extream loss margin is an additional calculated risk that can arise beyound the VaR based on historical data analysis.

 MTM: mark to market= sell-buy price

 SPAN Margin: the intial margin  required for the positions is computed using a software call SPAN(Standard Portfoli analysis of risk) and hence is also referred as SPAN margin.

 Exposure Margin: an aditional margin over and above the span margin is collected which is know as  the exposure margin.
